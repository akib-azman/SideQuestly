# SideQuestly
SideQuestly is a lightweight, single-page web app that turns real life into a game by generating random “side quests” you can complete anytime, anywhere.

Instead of endlessly scrolling, you press a button and get a small, structured challenge. Something creative, social, productive or exploratory to do in the real world.

Concept-
SideQuestly is built around a simple lookL
1. Generate a quest
2. Describe what to do with it
   - Accept
   - Reroll
   - Save for later
3. Complete it in real life

The goal is to introduce spontaneity, break routine and push users slightly outside their comfort zone in a controlled, fun way.

Features-
1. One-click quest generation
2. Structured quest cards with:
   - Title
   - Description
   - Difficulty
   - Catagory
   - Environment (Indoor/Outdoor/Mixed)
   - Time estimate
   - Optional twist

Difficulty system-
Three tiers of quests:
  1. Warmup Quests
     Low effort, quick, minimal discomfort
  2. Adventure Quests
     Mildly challanging, often involve movement or social interaction
  3. Legend Quests
     High effort, high discomfort, or memorable experiences

Filters-

Users can customize what kind of quests they receive:
  - Difficulty filter
  - Environment filter
  - Indoor
  - Outdoor
  - Mixed (default)
Filters directly affect quest generation.

Categories-

Each quest belongs to one or more categories:
  - Creativity
  - Exploration
  - Physical
  - Productivity
  - Social
This allows easy expansion and better organization of quests.
